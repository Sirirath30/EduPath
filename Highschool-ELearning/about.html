About EduPath

We believe every high school student deserves access to quality education. EduPath was built to bridge the gap between traditional schooling and modern, flexible learning.

Founder & Director

Mr. Samith Sirirath

With extensive experience in international education, Mr. Sirirath founded EduPath with a vision to make high-quality learning accessible to every student. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering and a Master’s degree in Cybersecurity from England. With a strong passion for technology and education, he is committed to integrating innovative solutions that enhance learning opportunities and empower students to achieve their full potential.

"Technology can level the playing field, and that's what EduPath is all about."
